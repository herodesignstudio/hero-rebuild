National Repertory Orchestra 09 Season Campaign

In the second year of our work for the NRO we pushed the design forward once again, to build on the success of the 2008 season. The primary figure, a violin, became the symbol of the season. It captured the vibrant beauty of the music, the orchestra and their home in Breckenridge, Colorado. A trio of illustrations, the violin, trumpet and french horn were featured throughout the season’s collateral and merchandise.
